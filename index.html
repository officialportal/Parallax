The Concept

Parallax represents the apparent displacement or the difference in the apparent direction of an object as seen from two different points of view.

Translating this visual distortion into an auditory experience, the project explores high-contrast soundscapes, heavy baselines, and clean aesthetics designed for the digital sub-culture.
